Festival novog filma i videa Alternative film/video biæe predstavljen na festivalu Les Inattendus u Lionu u Francuskoj, u subotu 26. januara, projekcijom nekih od nagradjenih radova na festivalu Alternative film/video 2006: CABINET Tima Šora (Tim Shore), AKCIJA (Lidija Antonoviæ), TO MOM (Dušan Gligorov).

 

 

Festival of new film and video ALTERNATIVE FILM/VIDEO will be presented on Saturday January 26, at the festival Les Inattendus, Lyon, France. Three awarded films from the List of significant acheivments of ALTERNATIVE FILM/VIDEO 2006 will be screened: CABINET (Tim Shore), AKCIJA (Lidija Antonoviæ), TO MOM (Dušan Gligorov).
